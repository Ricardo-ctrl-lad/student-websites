Top 3 smoothie recipes

~It is important to note that all of these smoothies serve four people and some may comntain nut milks howver these can be substituted with Soya milk or oat milks, enjoy.

BLUE MOON

.Place almond milk, half a bag of frozen blueberries, two dates, one very ripe banana, chia seeds and 1/2 a teaspoon of cinamon into your blender and blend until completely smooth.Then add your smoothie mixture into 4 cups and enjoy! If wanting to use this recipe for a smoothie boal add the mixture into 4 small boals and top with gronola and almond flakes to serve!

MORNING SUNSHINE

Add half a passionfruit, half a bag of frozen mango, 200ml of orange juice, tumeric and black pepper-trust!!blend until smooth and pour equally into 4 glasses. Or if wanting to turn this into a smoothie boal recipe add to 4 small boals and top with shredded cocnut and bannana.

milkshake smoothie

To create this milkshake smoothie add almond milk, honey/maple syrup, one banana, a 1/2 of a teaspoon of cinamon and one oragne peeled or a bag of frozen strawberries depending on what you fancy! blend these ingrediants until smooth before pouring equally into 4 glasses. If you crave a smoothie boal pour equally into 4 small boals and serve with crushed walnuts and chopped up dark chocolate!
