What is a Labubu?

Labubus are a type of plush toy character that gained popularity through their cute, quirky, and customizable design. The name "Labubu" comes from a blend of “love” and “bubu,” which means “cutie” in some languages, reflecting their adorable nature. These creatures started as a small project by a designer or artist who wanted to create something unique and fun for people to collect and personalize. The labubus were first introduced in online markets or at conventions, where their wide range of colors, designs, and sizes quickly attracted fans. Over time, they have evolved into a cultural phenomenon, often seen in various media and art forms, becoming beloved by collectors and enthusiasts alike.

Labubus are special because they’re cute, customizable, and fun to collect. You can personalize them with different colors and accessories, making each one unique.
