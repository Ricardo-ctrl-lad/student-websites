Romcoms were at their peak in the 2000's

27 DRESSES

Upbeat romcom about a woman who's always the bridesmaid, never the bride. Jane Nichols (Katherine Heigl) has led a selfless,romantic life, always putting others needs first and herself last,especially when it comes to marriage. With 27 bridesmaid dresses in her cupboard, Jane is beginning to see a pattern developing.

BRIDE WARS

Two best friends become rivals when they schedule their respective weddings on the same day.

HOW TO LOSE A GUY IN 10 DAYS

A ladies man bet his friends that he can get a woman to fall in love with him in 10 days, but unbeknownst to him, the woman he's dating is actually a magazine columnist working on a new column called "How to Lose a Guy in 10 Days", and she's doing everything she could to drive the guy she's dating crazy.
