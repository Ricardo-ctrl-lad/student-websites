Top 5 Movies

5: Jaws 1975

Jaws is an American thriller film, staring Roy Scheider. It is about when a massive killer shark unleashes chaos on a beach community off Long Island, it's up to a local sheriff, a marine biologist, and an old seafarer to hunt the beast down. You can watch it on Netflix.

4: Titanic 1997

Titanic is an American epic romantic disaster film, staring Kate Winslet and Leonardo DiCaprio. It is about when a poor artist and a rich debutante meet and fall in love on the famous voyage of the "unsinkable" RMS Titanic in 1912. You can watch it on Disney+.

3: Uncharted 2022

Uncharted is an American adventure action film, staring Tom Holland and Mark Wahlberg. It is about a group of treasure hunters who travel across the world to uncover various historical mysteries. You can watch it on Disney+.

2: Dirty Dancing 1987

Dirty Dancing is an American romantic drama dance film, staring Jennifer Grey and Patrick Swayze. It is about when she spended the summer at a Catskills resort with her family, the houseman falls in love with the camp's dance instructor. You can watch it on Hulu.

Sinister is a supernatural horror film, staring Ethan Hawke. It is about a contriversial true crime writer finds a box in his new home, revealing the murder case he is currently researching.
