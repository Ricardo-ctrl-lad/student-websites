Cutest cats

List of the top 5 most adorable cat breeds and best ones to live with.

Persian

Not only are they fluffy and adorable, Persian cats tend to be cuddly and friendly. When it comes to fuzzy kittens, the Persian is one of the most popular cats. You'll often find them patiently waiting for you to sit down so they can find their comfortable spot right on your lap. Once the petting session is over, they'll be able to steal your heart just by sitting quietly and looking adorable.

Ragdoll

When such a cute name is chosen for a whole breed of cats, you know there are plenty of cuddles to be had once you welcome a Ragdoll cat into your life. Commonly known as one of the friendliest cat breeds, Ragdolls love nothing more than to spend time with their owner even if that means following you around when you're too busy for hugs.

Maine Coon

The Maine Coon manages being the loyal companion you expect from a friendly cat breed while keeping their independent streak. Despite their big physique, they love nothing more than a good cuddle in your lap. The Maine Coon is a very intelligent cat, so keep the toy box topped up with plenty of puzzle toys; nothing spells fun for these kittens more than a good mental challenge.

Exotic Shorthair

The Exotic is closely related to the Persian and therefore borrows a lot of their quiet and sweet nature. They are known to love play time a bit more than a Persian and have considerably shorter hair, making it easier to manage their grooming schedule. If you are looking for a loyal companion, the Exotic is one of the most affectionate cat breeds you can welcome into your life.

Siamese

If you are looking for a social feline that loves a good chat, you will look forward spending time with a Siamese. These kittens know how to make their presence felt with their boisterous personality. They are beautiful and intelligent,and are also quite fond of humans. But be aware that they are not too keen on being left home alone - they need to be with family or at least with another fellow Siamese to be happy kittens.
