What are Labubus?

Labubu is a plush toy character created by Hong Kong designer Kasing Lung and introduced in 2015. Featuring a playful yet slightly fierce appearance—a round, furry body, wide eyes, pointed ears, and sharp teeth forming a mischievous smile—Labubu has gained significant popularity, especially among adult collectors. In 2019, Pop Mart began producing Labubu toys in various colors, shapes, and sizes, including plush and vinyl figures. The brand experienced a surge in demand in 2024, partly due to celebrity endorsements, notably from Blackpink's Lisa.

What makes them special?

Labubu's unique blend of playful mischief and artistic design has made it a cultural phenomenon. Its fusion of 'kawaii' aesthetics with subtle gothic elements appeals to a wide audience. The character's popularity has been further amplified by celebrity endorsements, notably from BLACKPINK's Lisa, and its presence in various Pop Mart collectible series.

Different types of labubus

Labubu, the mischievous plush character created by Kasing Lung, is available in a wide array of colors, each adding a unique flair to its playful design. Some of the notable colors include: Classic Brown: The original hue, offering a warm and traditional look. Light Brown: A softer shade, providing a gentle and approachable appearance. Purple: A vibrant tone that adds a touch of whimsy. White: A clean and minimalist variant, highlighting Labubu's features. Macaron Colors: Pastel shades inspired by macaron desserts, including pink, blue, and green, each bringing a sweet and delightful aesthetic. Coffee Color: A rich, warm shade reminiscent of a comforting beverage. Exciting Macaron Series: Features Labubu in macaron-inspired colors and designs, adding a sweet touch to the collection. These diverse color options allow fans and collectors to choose a Labubu that resonates with their personal style or to collect multiple versions for a more comprehensive display.
