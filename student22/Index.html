Location

Mauritius is a very unique country. The island is located in the Indian ocean, being off the spouthern-eastern coast of Africa.It is 2,040 km² and the capital,Port Loius, is 46.7 km². Because the island is so tiny, Mauritius is quite wealthy and has loads of tourists.

Religion

There are 52% Hindus, 28% Christians and 16% Muslim and is the only country in Africa as its main religion. Isn't that so interesting?

Ethnic groups

Mauritius is primarily made of Indo-Mauritians 68% and then Creoles 27%, Sino-Mauritians 3%, Franco-Mauritians 2%
