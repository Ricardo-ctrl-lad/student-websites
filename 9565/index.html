S/S '25 trend predictions'

colour pallete'

The upcoming seasons will be filled with soft, airy tones. Expect shades such as light pink, orange and yellow, and more muted shades such as baby blue, lavender, navy, and beige.

style trend: trenchoats'

Trenchcoats are the perfect spring staple, and best for transitioning weather and seasons. Perfect tokeep warm in the UK's weather surprises. From beige to navy, to baby pink a trenchcoat is essential to add to your collection. Shop from high- end stores such as Burberry or mid market stores such as Zara, H&M, or M&S.

style trend: dresses/skirtsover jeans'

More of an out-there statement trend reemerging is dresses or skirts over jeans. Popular in the late 00's and early 2010's especially in kids shows on nickelodeon and disney. For good flared or skinny jeans shop from the thrift, or H&M. Cute mini dresses can be sourced from Zara or stradivarius and skirts from bershka or thrifted.
