The Basics

First of all, you will need a journal. One of the most popular brands, Archer and Olive are known for their great quality and beautiful designs, with one journal costing an average of £30. If this is too far out of your budget, there are many cheaper alternatives such as the LEUCHTTURM journal costing £16.95, or even making your own. As well as this, you will also need some pens and highlighters, i recommend the zebra mildliners.

The Contents

After collecting your supplies, you will need to start writing in your journal. Most commonly, these journals are used for mplanning your days in the months, helping to organise your life events. This will usually come wiht a mood tracker, where at the end of the day you fill in a picture corresponding with the day, with a certain colour that represents your overall mood throughout the day. Each month usually has a different theme, usually matching a certain holiday in that month, with a title page showing the general aaestehtic of the month.
