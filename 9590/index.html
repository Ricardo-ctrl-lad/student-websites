BONES tv show

2005-2017

Bones >

Bones it a tv show that ran from 2005-2017 that starred David Boreanaz and Emily Deschanel. Inspired off the life of renouned forensic anthropologist Kathy Reichs. The main character, Temperance Brennan, who was named after one of Kathy Reich's protaganist in her crime novels,and her forensic team consult with Special Agent Seeley Booth of the FBI to solve murder cases, whilst trying to ignore the budding romance between Booth and Temperance until they can't anymore. Whilst solving case after case, the team face constant twists and turns that reveal a dangerous past, growing vendettas that will end no other way than death and gruesome murder after murder but still have a laugh. Consisting of 12 seasons, the programme showcasese the true side of Forensic Anthropology, Forensic Pathology, Forensic Art and Police work with humour. However, this show features varying levels of gore that are not for the squeamish.

Bones killers

Obviously as the show has 12 seasons there are many many killers but a few stand out such as Gormagon, Gravedigger, Howard Epps, The Ghost Killer, Christopher Pelant and Jacob Broadsky. These killers and some more had multiple episodes centered around their crimes, most took a sepcific interest in the team but all wwere criminal masterminds and sadists.

Gravedigger

The Gravedigger would kidnap victims for a ransom but with a sick twist. She would place them in a container underground with only enough air for 12 hours and if the ransom was not paid their location would never be found. After the team got too closde to catching her, she kidnapped Temperance and one of he team, Jack Hodgins. She was active for years and infamous but she was eventually caught after wreaking more havoc. She was killed however by a vigilante sniper killer known as Jacob Broadsky, who trained Booth to be a marine sniper, starting off the hunt for him.

Gormogon

Gormogon while arguably the most horrific is also possibly the most referred to killer in the entire series with a case stretching over multiple seasons. In a deep conspiracy theory based group with cannabilistic tendencies, teacher-mentor killing partners gowing back generations whwere comitting atrocious acts. They were only discovered to be active after a coincedental discovery of a skull. This case infected one of Brennan's own team, Dr. Zack Addy, in an emotion rollercoaster of an episode.

Christopher Pelant

Christopher Pelant is a hacker and poetic serial killer with a deep obsession with ruining the romance between Booth ans Temperance. Pelant hacks into secure databases for pure chaos but as a master manipulator he face little to none consequences. After being exposed as the hacker he is, he turns into a revende-driven killer. He finds inspiration from famous literature that he knows will have the ultimate shockfactor. He was only caught after three long seasons where he was the main antagonist.
