5 High potential

High Potential is a Tv-show based on a French version called HPI. It is about a mother who has exeptional brain and gets hired by the LAPD. She solves cases with a new partner while also trying to dicover what happened to her husband who went missing 15 years prior. Stream on disney + .

4 Gilmore Girls

Gilmore Girls is a comedy tv-show with bits of romance and drama. The shoe follows the lives of the main characters Rory and Loreli gilmore( mother and daughter) . Its shows loreli advancing through her work as a hotel manager and rory facing of the troubled of high school then college. It has 7 seasons and a spinoff series about them when they are older.

3 The vampire Diaries

The Vampire Diaries is set in the fictional town of Mystic Falls, Virginia, a town with a supernatural past. The story follows the life of Elena Gilbert ,a teenager who lost her parents in a car crash, whos world changes when new boys Stefan and Damon Salvatore , 160 and 170 year old vampires, show her the secret past of her town. The Vampire Diaries doesn't have a specific genre and is really just a mix of them all with love triangles and supernatural people everywhere.

2 Greys anatomy

Greys anatomy is a medical and romantic drama centered around Meredith Grey, an aspiring surgeon and daughter of one of the best surgeons, Dr. Ellis Grey and the interns she works with from the beginning . Throughout the series, Meredith goes through professional and personal challenges along with fellow surgeons at Seattle Grace Hospital It follows the personal and work life of interns, residents and attendings. It has 21 seasons and is still on going .

1 Friends

Friends is a 90s comedy about 6 friends( Rachel, Monica, Phoebe, Chandler, Joey and Ross) who go through just about every life experience imaginable together; divorce, love, heartbreak, children, fights, job losses, new jobs and lots more drama throughout all 10 seasons.
